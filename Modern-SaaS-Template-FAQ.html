General
What is Aura exactly?
Aura is a unified operating system for modern workflows. We combine project management, automation, and analytics into a single, cohesive dashboard to help teams build faster.
Is there a mobile app available?
Yes! We have native apps for iOS and Android. You can manage your projects, receive notifications, and reply to comments directly from your phone.
Billing & Subscription
Can I change my plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the account settings. Prorated charges will be calculated automatically.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not satisfied, simply contact support within the first month.
Technical Support
How do I access the API?
API access is available on Pro and Enterprise plans. You can generate your API keys in the Developer Settings section of your dashboard.
Is my data secure?
Security is our top priority. We use end-to-end encryption, regular audits, and are SOC2 Type II compliant to ensure your data remains safe.
