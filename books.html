What You’ll Find Here

The fiction titles presented by Kingdom & Quill are written by T. S. Telles and crafted to stand shoulder-to-shoulder with mainstream entertainment in the romance and fantasy genres. Though designed to entertain, these stories carry themes that naturally resonate with the Christian community and, out of respect for the convictions behind this project, they avoid explicit sexual content and any values that clash with a life of faith. You won’t find identity-driven manifestos or crude language here. What you’ll find are deep narratives—plots that balance tension and purpose without losing their edge.

If you came for romance, suspense, or fantasy, you’re in the right place. Check each book’s content rating and its availability in your preferred language.

Sit back. Explore. And let each story speak for itself. God bless you.

Part 1: Born Ricciwood

Genre: Contemporary Romance, Christian Romance, Urban Romance, Emotional Drama, Crime Fiction, Fan Fiction
Language: English (US), Portuguese (Brazil)

Gabriel Ricciwood has always been a mystery—even to himself. Haunted by tragedy, carrying the legacy of a last name no one dares speak aloud, Gabriel steps into Sant’Hale, an elite Manhattan school where silence is survival and history is rewritten to serve the powerful.

He doesn’t belong.

Not with the rich heirs of the Council. Not with the perfect girls. Not with the stories they tell of his grandfather. But beneath the marble halls and well-dressed lies, Gabriel finds something he never expected: truth, loyalty, faith—and maybe even love. Between whispers of betrayal and the scars of a past he didn’t choose, Gabriel must decide who he is, what he believes, and how far he’s willing to go to protect the people he’s learning to care about.

This book is recommended for readers aged 16 and older due to mature themes.

O Poder dos Quatro Tronos

Genre: Fantasy, Adventure, Fiction, Horror, Thriller, Terror, Romance
Language: Português (BR)

Assim como o poder dos deuses, há muito tempo o tecido que unia os bruxos e as criaturas mágicas foi corrompido. Um reino que antes vivia em harmonia mergulhou na escuridão, dividido pela obsessão de um rei.

Durante uma era inteira, um homem perverso governou o Grande Norte com mentiras e falsas narrativas. Bruxos com poderes incompreendidos foram escravizados e punidos por usar sua magia — não para dominar, mas para sobreviver.

Neste primeiro livro da saga Axwar, Alyce é despertada por um chamado antigo: ela carrega um poder destinado a restaurar a justiça e reinar com sabedoria sobre os oprimidos. Mas num mundo atemporal, repleto de bruxos, dragões, caçadores, vampiros e criaturas sombrias, sua maior batalha não será contra monstros… e sim contra os rostos familiares que escondem traições.

Para herdar o trono, Alyce precisará distinguir aliados de inimigos — antes que seja tarde demais.

Este livro é recomendado para leitores com 18 anos ou mais, devido a temas maduros e cenas de violência intensa.
