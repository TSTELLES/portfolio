Music that accompanies those who choose change.

“Reborn in Christ” is not just music—it is a manifesto of faith. These songs were written for people who wish to live the true Gospel, who want a new identity built on the truth of salvation and to bring the name of the Lord to all who haven't heard.

The Emotional Bridge: Book & Music

Reborn in Christ brings the prayers from the universe of Welcome to New York as declarations of truth—about who God is and who we are in Him. Here, emotions and pain are transformed into strength, faith, and identity.

The album gathers eight tracks that combine an urban atmosphere, epic energy, and emotional intensity, always sustained by clear, clean, and encouraging Christian messages.

If your faith is alive, if you have chosen to follow Christ in motion, this sound is for you. And if you are truly reborn, feed your mind and spirit with words that come from the heart and point directly to the Lord.
